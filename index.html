HOW TO MAKE A DELICIOUS BROWNIE

Hello, This is a tutorial on how to make a delicious brownie that wil melt in your mouth. I promise, it will be chewy, sweet and obviously amazing. I am going to go step by step to show you how to make a delicious brownie. This brownie serves 16 and takes 30 mins in total to make

INGREDIENTS

2 cups white sugar
1 1/2 cups flour
1 cup butter (melted)
1 tsp vamilla extract
1/2 tsp baking powder
4 eggs
1/2 cup cocoa powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup walnut (optional)
