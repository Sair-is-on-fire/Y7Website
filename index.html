HOW TO MAKE A DELICIOUS BROWNIE

Hello and welcome to this tutorial on how to make world class brownies
I promise it wil be fudgy, chewy and overall amazing. Read on.

INGREDIENTS

2 cups white sugar
1 1/2 cups flour
1 cup butter (melted)
1 tsp vamilla extract
1/2 tsp baking powder
4 eggs
1/2 cup cocoa powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup walnut (optional)
1 cup chcolate chips (ptional)

EQIPMENT

Hand mixer/Stand Mixer
Oven
Baking tray
Baking sheet
Bowl
Spatula

METHOD

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F and put your baking sheet on the
baking tray

Step 2

Slowly incorporate your melted butter,eggs,vanila extract,sugar and salt
After combined ad in the rest of the ingredients. After fully incorporated
put it on your baking tray and if you want to decorate with walnuts

Step 3

Bake in preheated pan until top has dried and sides have strated to pull
away from the sides of the pan, after 20-30 minutes poke a toothpick
If your toothpick is dry take your brownie out if wet let it bake some
more. Cool before slcing into squares.
