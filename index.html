HOW TO MAKE A DELICIOUS BROWNIE

Hello, This is a tutorial on how to make a delicious brownie that wil melt in your
mouth. I promise, it will be chewy, sweet and obviously amazing.I am going
to go step by step to show you how to make a delicious brownie. This brownie
serves 16 and takes 30 mins in total to make

INGREDIENTS

2 cups white sugar
1 1/2 cups flour
1 cup butter (melted)
1 tsp vamilla extract
1/2 tsp baking powder
4 eggs
1/2 cup cocoa powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup walnut (optional)

EQIPMENT

Hand mixer/Stand Mixer
Oven
Baking tray
Baking sheet
Bowl
Spatula

METHOD

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F and put your baking sheet on the
baking tray

Step 2

Slowly incorporate your melted butter,eggs,vanila extract,sugar and salt
After combined ad in the rest of the ingredients. After fully incorporated
put it on your baking tray and if you want to decorate with walnuts
